Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • We don’t collect names, emails, or other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to operate and maintain the Bot, enable features, and perform moderation functions. Your data will not be sold or shared without your consent.

3. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account uses the Bot. If you leave or request deletion, it will be removed. Open a support ticket to request data deletion.

4. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion

5. Data Sharing

We only share data when required by law or if you explicitly allow it.

6. Security

We use encryption and access restrictions to protect your data, though no system is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in our support server, and continued use of the Bot indicates agreement.

8. Contact Us

Open a support ticket for any privacy-related questions or concerns.
